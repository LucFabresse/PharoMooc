Pharo (http://pharo.org) is a pure dynamic and reflective object programming language inspired by Smalltalk. Pharo offers a powerful environment with unique features (https://pharo.org/features). You can see some success stories at: (http://pharo.org/success). Join the community (https://pharo.org/community).
